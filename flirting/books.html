    How to Attract Anyone, Anytime, Anyplace : The Smart Guide to Flirting
    by Susan Rabin, Barbara J. Lagowski

    This book is an easy read and I found it to be fun and full of excellent examples. It will also show you what not to do and why. I helped me a lot with working with people as well as meeting them for romantic reasons. The big lesson for me is that the people I'm dealing with may have friends or siblings looking to meet someone. Better to be nice than not!


    Flirting for Success : The Art of Building Rapport
    by Jill Speiegel

    I haven't read this one yet, however, it shows that flirting can open than just romantic for your life. The book also goes into more depth about building immediate trust, and gaining confidence for business and personal success. I may have to go get this one!


    How to Attract Money
    by Joseph Murphy

    I have read this quick read and for about $3.00 explains tactics to make money and other rewards come your way. Also, it shows the value of flirting and enjoying getting to know others.
