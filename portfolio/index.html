 BIG WOOFIE STUDIOS 
 

These games were built in 1997-1998 to showcase the interactivity of the WebTV platform, exhibiting the combination of Macromedia Flash, interactive midi and .wav playback, and the WebTV multimedia language extensions. Microsoft immediately approved of Minesweeper once the acquisition was complete.

Thanks to ruffle.js for making flash files playable once again, you can see what was Silicon Valley cutting-edge web interactivity served over a copper telephone line to the WebTV box in the living room attached to a TV. Best enjoyed airplayed full screen to a TV.
