Create your own show at Tune In Now!
Ever wanted to be a DJ? Ever wanted to tell the world about your favorite songs in a caffeinated rage? Ever wanted to break some local talent out to the rest of the world cause you thought they were really cool?

Now is your chance! At TuneInNow, you can create your own hour long show. All you need to do is choose songs either from our library or yours, add some juicy tidbits about the bands, and you can turn your music thesis into a radio show for extra credit!

To create a show, you actually create a playlist of separate sound files and order them the way you want them. These sound files can come either from our library of songs and audio clips or from you.

The sound files from you can be songs or your own commentary. You may wish to read our policy about what constitutes acceptable sound files that are uploaded from your machine.

The total length of all these files should be no more than one hour. The play list will show you how long your show plays as you add files.

The sound files should be in .wav, .aiff, or .mp3 formats.

Any questions, just ask!
