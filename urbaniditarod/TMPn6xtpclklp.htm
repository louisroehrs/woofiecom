San Francisco Urban Iditarod!

 What it is!

The San Francisco Urban Iditarod runs a dog-cart race through the city once a year as a salute to the Alaskan Iditarod dog sled race from Anchorage to Nome. Here in SF, "dogs" pull "sleds" from one "watering hole" to the next for a total of three miles. Now, substitute person for dog, shopping cart for sled, and local bar for watering hole. In 1999, the Big Woofie supported the Tiki Dogs team. In 2000, the Big Woofie ran with the Los Dingos.
