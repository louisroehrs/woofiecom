Welcome!

We investigate human-computer interaction in the Silicon Valley. Our principal focus is understanding the effect the "valley lifestyle" on the sociological structure of modern day culture and the possible transformations during the next millenium.

This research will include:
  • The effects of the 20 hour workday on family life.
  • The symbiosis of successful capitalists and those who spend their lives working for them.
  • Life regeneration activities including but not limited to travel, music, and petting cats.
  • Sleep deprivation on humans, pets, and machinery.
  • Human-human interaction and the disappearance of the front porch society.
  • Comparisons to sociological climates such as that found in Sweden.
