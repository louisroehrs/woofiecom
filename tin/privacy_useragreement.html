PlanetAll seeks to build community in a highly fragmented world. Since PlanetAll handles a great deal of personal information, we have gone to great lengths to ensure that all of your information is private. To this end, we'd like to highlight several important privacy features:

PlanetAll will never sell or otherwise distribute user information to direct marketers or any other parties without the specific consent of the user. As a free service that depends on advertising revenue to survive, PlanetAll does reserve the right to collect and distribute aggregate information (age distribution, gender distribution, etc.)

User Control of Information

PlanetAll allows members a high degree of control over their information. PlanetAll members set separate permissions for each contact in the system. As such, members can build their lists of contacts while protecting their privacy. To set permissions on your information, visit the contacts section.

Data Security

Our firewall and databases (set up by a security specialist who set up the firewall for the Federal Reserve) are organized in such as way as to prevent the theft or corruption of information. Users are required to log on to PlanetAll using a confidential and unique password.

PlanetAll was created to help people keep in touch and stay in touch with people and groups important to them. PlanetAll is home to a great diversity of individuals, and as such we try and make it a place that is safe for all members while enabling individual freedom of expression and providing tools for organizing and communication. Promotes or contains bigotry, racism, hatred, profanity or obscenity; Promotes physical harm against any group or individual; Deliberately disseminates false or misleading information; Exists primarily for commercial purposes.
