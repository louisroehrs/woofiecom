Welcome to Word Finder!

The object of this game is to find words hidden within the word finder grid. Words can be found horizontally, vertically, diagonally, backwards and forwards. To select a word, simply click on the word's first and last letters.

When you have found a correct word it will highlight in yellow. Incorrect words will not highlight.

Select 'Hint' to briefly highlight a letter within an unfound word. Select 'Solve' to reveal the puzzle's solution.
