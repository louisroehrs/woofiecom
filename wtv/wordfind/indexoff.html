How to Play
 
  		• The object of this game is to find a famous phrase hidden in the wordfind grid.
  		• Each phrase winds through the wordfind like a snake.
  		• To select a word simply click on the word's first and last letter. Then, look for the next word in the phrase. It always begins in a square adjacent to the last letter of the word you just found.
  		• Words can be found in any sequence. It is OK if you find the last word in the phrase before you find the first.
