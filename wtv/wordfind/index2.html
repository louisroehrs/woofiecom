How to Play

 
  		• Words can be found horizontally, vertically, diagonally, backwards and forwards.
  		• Twenty seconds will be added to the wordfind clock for each correct word you find.
  		• Clicking on "Hint" will briefly highlight a letter within a word you have not yet found. Five seconds will be deducted from the wordfind clock each time you click on "Hint."
  		• If you are stumped click on "Solve" and the puzzle's solution will be revealed.
  		• Good Luck!
