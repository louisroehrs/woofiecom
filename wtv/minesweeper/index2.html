How to Play


  		• Numbers indicate how many adjacent squares contain mines. For example, revealing a '2' means that there are mines in two empty adjacent squares.
  		• In order to save you time, selecting a cell with a '0' will automatically reveal all other empty adjacent squares.
  		• You can choose from three difficulty levels. 'Private' is the easiest, 'Major' is harder, and 'General' is the most difficult.
